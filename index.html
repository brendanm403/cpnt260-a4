Discover The Desert

Fossil Excavation Tour

8am - 10am 10am - 12pm

Join us on an exciting expedition to the rich fossil deposits of Hoenn's Desert! Upon Arrival at the information center, our team will transport you to the excavation sites located deep within Route 111. First, our expert archaeologists will demonstrate how fossil deposits are located and how the fossils themselves are excavated! After the demonstration its your turn!

Due to the high durability and strength of the armor and skull fossils we are proud to let all visitors do a first hand excavation! All fossils that get excavated are swiftly packaged and shipped to Rustburo city for revival at the Devon Corporation headquarters! This is a vital program for ensuring that pokemon fossil species are revived and can fill there niche in the ecosystem! We hope to see you soon!

Pokemon Safari

6pm - 8pm 8pm - 10pm

Strap in for a once in a lifetime ecotourism experience! The night is when the local fauna spring to life and we want you to experience it with us! Visitors will depart from the information center with our expert biologists there to guide every step of the way! After a short ride from the center you'll be immersed in Hoenn's wildest frontier!

The desert is home to many species of pokemon waiting for you to see! Our tour guides will get you up close for amazing sights! If you're lucky maybe even some interactions with the more docile species that call the desert home! This is a great educational program for all and a critical operation in generating funds for the future preservation of this wonderful biomes ecology. Can't wait to meet you!
